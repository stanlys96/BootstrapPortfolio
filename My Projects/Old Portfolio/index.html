Pig Game

This is a project I created with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. This is an interactive game. Two players get to roll the dice to get a random number. If he gets a number from 2 to 6, it will be added to his total score. If he gets 1, automatically change to the other player. Press hold to hold your score, and it will be the other player's turn. The first one to reach 100 is the winner.
